You can donate blood:

  1. If you are over 50 kg
  2. Starting at 17 years of age and can enroll as a first time donor up to 65
  3. If you have had something to eat and drink before donating.
  4. Four months after having a tattoo or piercing.
  5. Six months after having a baby
  6. If you are a smoker
  7. Even if you don't know your blood group
